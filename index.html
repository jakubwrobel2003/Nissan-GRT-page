Nissan GT-R

Nissan GT-R - Nissan's two-door coupé, announced on December 6, 2007. It went on sale internationally in early June 2008. In late 2010, it underwent a facelift. In 2009, the car won the title of World Performance Car of the Year.

GTR ENGINE

  • Maks. moment obrotowy - 632 Nm przy 3300-5800 obr./min
  • Przyspieszenie 0-100 km/h: 2,7 s
  • Prędkość maksymalna: 315 Km/h
